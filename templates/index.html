Topic 7 — Digital Signatures & Applications

Digital Signature Tool

Sign PDF documents with RSA/ECDSA, verify integrity, and demonstrate tamper detection — even a single byte change is caught.

✍️ Start Signing 🔍 Verify Signature ⚔️ Attack Demo

Sign Documents

Generate RSA-PSS or ECDSA key pairs and sign PDF files. The signed bundle contains the document, signature, and public key.

Verify Integrity

Upload a signed bundle to verify the signature. The tool recomputes SHA-256 and checks against the original — binary-level integrity.

Tamper Detection

Demonstrate how even 1 byte of modification is instantly detected. See the avalanche effect in SHA-256 with character-level hash diff.
